Introduction

Calzone is a delightful Italian dish that brings the flavors of pizza into a portable, hand-held form. It's perfect for a quick meal or a cozy night in. Let's get started on making this delicious treat. I have another night of working the docks after, so we're in a hurry.

Alright, let's roll up our sleeves and get cooking. Grab your ingredients and let's make some calzones.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110째F or 43째C)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled Italian sausage (optional)
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce (for dipping)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture. Knead until a smooth dough forms. Cover and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
  4. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a circle about 6 inches in diameter.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and cooked sausage (if using). Spoon the filling onto one half of each dough circle.
  6. Fold the other half over the filling and press the edges to seal. Use a fork to crimp the edges for a decorative touch.
  7. Place the calzones on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the tops with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  8. Bake for about 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping.
