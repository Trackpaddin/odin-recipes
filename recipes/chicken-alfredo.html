Chicken Alfredo

Photo credit: Viv Lynch on Flickr. Used with a Creative Commons license.

Introduction

When I think of home, I think back when I went to Lala's over in Newport. This dish is a callback to those days, with a creamy sauce and tender chicken that always makes you smile no matter how rotten the days of working the docks were. It's good eating.

You gotta start with the basics. Let's go.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh parsley sprig (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Remove from skillet and let rest.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and add minced garlic. Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream to the skillet and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and add to the sauce, tossing to coat.
  6. Slice the chicken and serve it on top of the pasta. Garnish with fresh parsley.
